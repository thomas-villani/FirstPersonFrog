HOW TO PLAY

Goal — hop across every lane to the far median. Each crossing ramps the level: more lanes, faster cars.

Controls

  • W A S D or arrows — hop forward, left, back, right (relative to where you face)
  • Mouse — look around
  • Click or Space — flick your tongue (catch bugs for XP)
  • F — Frog Focus slow-mo (once unlocked)
  • Shift + move — long jump (once Hip Hopping is unlocked)
  • 1–4 — pick a skill when offered after a frog level-up
  • Esc pause · M mute

Stay alive — wheels kill, but the gap between front and back wheels is survivable. You can hop into a truck's belly and out the other side. Once you commit to a hop you can't change your mind, so wait for openings.

Score — chain near-misses (THREADED, GRAZED) to build your combo multiplier. Crossing a level untouched grants a bonus. Five lives total.
